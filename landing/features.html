All Features

Everything you need to create, manage, and send professional invoices.

🎨

6 Professional Templates

Choose from Modern, Classic, Minimal, Bold, Warm, and Slate. Each template is fully styled with unique accent colors, typography, and layout — switch between them with a single click without losing your content.

📄

PDF Export & Print

Export any invoice or receipt to a professional PDF using html2pdf. Print directly from the browser with print-specific styles that hide the editor chrome and render only the document.

👥

Client Address Book

Save client details (name, company, email, phone, address) and fill invoices instantly via the client picker dropdown. Edit and delete clients from the client grid.

📊

Dashboard Analytics

View key metrics at a glance: total revenue, outstanding invoices, paid this month, and overdue amounts. Recent invoices table with quick edit, duplicate, and delete actions.

🧮

Tax & Discounts

Add tax rates (percentage) and discounts (percentage or flat amount) to any invoice. Multi-currency support with 8 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, AED, and INR.

📱

Invoices & Receipts

Toggle between invoice and receipt mode. Receipts omit the due date and use "Receipt" heading — perfect for payments already collected.

🔢

Custom Invoice Numbering

Configure your own invoice number prefix and starting number. Preview updates live as you type. Numbers auto-increment with each new invoice.

🖼

Logo Support

Upload your business logo (PNG/SVG) in the editor or set a default in settings. The logo appears on the invoice header and is included in PDF exports.

🔍

Search & Filter

Search invoices by number, client name, or company. Filter by status (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue) to quickly find what you need.
